01 / Who we are

"A.F.L.D. Almighty Forgives Lost Don't"

In the roaring midst of the asphalt symphony, the Lost MC was born from the spirit of six defiant souls who'd lost their path. Names like Lereenshawdesip, Triniti Mafiozo, Bobon Guerra, Alexander Romanov, and Ahmed Negan intertwined their fates.

The tale of the Lost MC echoes throughout the streets of Los Santos. A club that almost shook the very foundations of the city, its members facing deportation and lifelong sentences.
